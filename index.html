Fragments

Not an explanation. More like a set of coordinates.

Where am I?

A place built from artifacts: images, objects, electronics, memories—reassembled into comfort. The outside is quiet. The inside is opal.

Who are you?

VOIDTYPE is a brand, a practice, and a story engine. Upcycled tech. Handmade garments. Open-source process. A constellation of identities—held together by empathy.

What is this place?

It’s a gallery, a workshop, and a portal into Her Opaline Empathy. If you want proof, you’ll get it. If you want lore, you’ll drown in it.

Why are you doing this?

Because comfort is armor. Because trauma is real. Because recycling can be sacred. Because “learn it yourself” is the only way to keep your sovereignty.

When did you realize?

The moment the shards started reflecting back. The moment the void stopped feeling empty. The moment art became a way to return something to the world—cleaner than you found it.
